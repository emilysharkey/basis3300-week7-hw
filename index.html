Student Lifestyles Survey

Welcome to our survey on undergraduate student lifestyles! We appreciate your participation in this study, which aims to gather important information about your daily routines, habits, and health-realted behaviors. By answering the questions in this survey, you will help us better understand the diverse lifestyles of undergraduate students, and identify areas where we can improve their well-being. Your participation is voluntary and all responses will be kept confidential. We estimate that the survey will take approximately 10-15 minutes to complete. Thank you for your time and contribution to this important research!
